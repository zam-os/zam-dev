---
title: Learning is most effective during real work
created: 2026-03-28
---

We believe that lasting skills are built by doing real tasks, not by studying in
isolation. A developer who installs Homebrew to solve an actual problem retains
that knowledge far longer than one who reads a tutorial.

ZAM is built on this premise: the learning session and the work session are the
same session. The tool should be nearly invisible.
