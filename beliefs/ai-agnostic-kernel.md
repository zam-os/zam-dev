---
title: The learning engine must be AI-agnostic
created: 2026-03-28
---

We believe the FSRS scheduler, the token model, and the review protocol belong
to no single AI provider. Any AI CLI — Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Copilot, or
something built next year — should be able to drive a ZAM session through the
bridge protocol.

The kernel has zero LLM dependencies. It is pure learning science. The AI is
an interchangeable interface, not a dependency.
